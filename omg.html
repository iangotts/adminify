How it works

Three steps. Zero writes to your org.

OMG is read-only. Connect once, run the analysis, get the briefings. Your org is the source — never the target.

01 · CONNECT
OAuth into your org.
A read-only connection over the Metadata, Tooling, and REST APIs. No writes, no commits, no managed package. Disconnect any time.
02 · ANALYSE
Build the graph.
OMG ingests every object, field, flow, trigger, profile, integration, Data 360 model, and Agentforce agent — and links them into one queryable graph with AI on top.
03 · ASK
From Slack or Claude.
Connect OMG to your team's Slack and your Claude account. Ask anything. Get briefings, dependency maps, risk lists, and engagement plans — formatted, cited, ready to share.
